Academic resources created by Dr. Prajwal Dange for ENT and Head & Neck Surgery trainees.

## DNB ENT Theory Q&A

A comprehensive question-and-answer resource for the **DNB ENT Theory Examination**, covering all 883 questions from 2011 to 2025 across all 4 papers. Answers are based on Scott-Brown's Otorhinolaryngology, Head and Neck Surgery, 8th Edition.

[Visit DNB ENT Theory Q&A](https://dangeprajwal.github.io/DNB-ENT-Theory-QA/)
